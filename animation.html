The Special moves of Jizard the Wizard

Jizard is unique when it comes to his physical body.

Jizard can walk as normal as a human, but he can also do the exact opposite. Whenever he is practicing spells smoke emit from his hands. He can start a flame in his palms. He can also hover over the ground opposed to walking.
